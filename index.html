Our Logos

Our logo has been specifically created for IntelligenceBank. It includes a distinctive Sun symbol that sits at the end of the horizontal IntelligenceBank brandmark or centered above the vertical brandmark. The IntelligenceBank wordmark which utilizes 2 greys simplifies legibility and reflects the brand personality: intelligent, friendly, creative, fun, human.
